About Tier 0.5

The new Tier 0.5 (Prompt Security) layer analyzes prompts before they reach the LLM:

  • PromptStructureValidator: Analyzes intent, PII, sensitivity, entities
  • PromptInjectionValidator: Detects jailbreaks, SQL/XSS, role-play attacks
  • Early Exit: Blocks clearly malicious prompts before expensive validation

Decision Types

  • ALLOW: Safe content
  • BLOCK: Dangerous content blocked
  • REGENERATE: Content needs improvement
  • ABSTAIN: Unable to determine
